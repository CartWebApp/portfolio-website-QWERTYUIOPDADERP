I was tasked as part of a small team of four people to make a website and app for the supercar company Trion Supercars. I worked as a web developer and designer. The website was made to work on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.

Before working on the project a competitive audit was commenced, gaining insights on the websites of other supercar companies.

The website operated HTML, CSS, and JS. It used localstorage to simulate a cart and store. We worked with client requests to create a website and app to their satisfaction.
